## Lore

The world has been destroyed by antimatter monsters ! Well not entirely! One small village of indomitable ~~Gauls~~ matter robots still holds out against the invaders 😉.

You first start in a little base left from the Cataclysm. You have a little power generator, but you are going to need way more to build your factory and
even go to other dimensions.

- If you want more information about producing energy: `Producing Energy`
- If you want more information about the tech tree: check `Tech tree`
- If you want more information about the monsters: check `Monsters`

Use strange weapons to defend against those pesky anti-birds, collect some crazy strange resources from antimatter bases, and (maybe?) find the root of the Cataclysm !

### Producing energy

When you start the game, you have an energy collector, which helps you collect energy from space's vacuum.
It's not very fast, but you need to start somewhere. Note that it also expels a bit of pale red antimatter.

### Turrets

- Antimatter repeller: Repels black antimatter around him using electricity, usefull for gaining terrain
(Costs iron to build)
- Matter missiles: Places a repeller on a drone, and disables the repeller near the target (monster), because the monster is antimatter and the drone is matter, everything explodes
(Costs iron to build, and fuel + repeller for operating)

### Monsters

The monsters are made of antimatter. They are pretty strong, because when they touch any of your infrastructure, both the monster and a part of your base explodes

### Types of antimatter

- Redish antimatter - Attracts monsters, the more red, the more attractive,
It can even give speed boosts / strength...
- Black antimatter - Nearly everywhere after the Cataclysm, you can't see through it,
and you will want to get rid of it !
- Green antimatter - Can be used to make energy
